# Vor

## Description
Vor is an AI-assisted hiring operations platform that connects to your ATS, triangulates candidate data from resumes, LinkedIn profiles, and interview transcripts, and supports a real-world two-interviewer workflow before generating a structured evaluation scorecard you can push back to the ATS. Each job requisition can be staffed by a recruiter and a senior developer who collaborate through separate interview steps, role-specific inputs, and a shared final recommendation.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Disconnected Candidate Data
Recruiters piece together information scattered across PDFs, LinkedIn, ATS records, and interview notes. Vor ingests and merges all sources into a single unified candidate profile automatically.

### Problem 2: Time-Consuming Interview Prep Across Different Interviewers
Preparing targeted interview questions requires manually comparing resumes against job requirements, and the questions needed by HR are different from the ones needed by a technical interviewer. Vor generates role-aware briefings that help recruiters run the HR screening and help senior developers run the technical interview.

### Problem 3: Multi-Step Interview Synthesis Burnout
After separate HR and technical interviews, teams must reconstruct what was said in each conversation, reconcile both perspectives, and turn fragmented notes into a final evaluation. Vor analyzes each transcript independently, asks role-specific follow-up questions, and combines both interview streams into a single structured scorecard ready for the ATS.

### Problem 4: Poor Evaluation Data Quality
Rushed manual notes lead to inconsistent, incomplete, and unstructured feedback that's hard to act on. Vor produces standardized markdown scorecards with evidence-backed summaries, quoted transcript excerpts, separate HR and technical signals, and clear hiring recommendations.

### Problem 5: No Visibility Into Interviewer Capacity
Admins need to know who is available to take new requisitions and how many active hiring processes each recruiter and senior developer already owns. Vor provides assignment and workload visibility so teams can distribute interviews intentionally instead of relying on ad hoc coordination.

## Key Features
- ATS integration to import job requisitions and candidates
- Three foundational roles: Admin, Recruiter, and Senior Developer
- Role-based permissions so each person only edits the parts of the workflow they own
- Team assignment per job requisition with optional recruiter and optional senior developer, but at least one assigned owner
- Multi-source candidate data triangulation (ATS record, resume PDF, LinkedIn profile)
- Recruiter-owned candidate enrichment tasks including ATS validation, resume upload, and LinkedIn attachment
- AI-generated interview briefings with targeted questions oriented separately to HR screening and technical evaluation
- Separate transcript upload and analysis for HR interview and technical interview
- Active recall questionnaires tailored to the recruiter and the senior developer respectively
- Structured markdown scorecard generation that synthesizes HR and technical interview evidence into one final recommendation
- Admin view for interviewer directory, availability, and active process load
- One-click scorecard push back to the ATS
